# Psyflo - AI Mental Health Companion

An intelligent mental health companion app that provides 24/7 accessible wellness support through AI-driven mood tracking, personalized recommendations, and privacy-first design.

## 🎯 Mission

Making mental health support affordable, accessible, and stigma-free for everyone—especially students and young professionals navigating stress, anxiety, and mental wellness challenges.

## 💡 Problem We're Solving

Mental health support is critical, yet many people lack access to affordable, accessible resources. Barriers include:
- High costs ($100-200 per therapy session)
- Limited professional availability
- Stigma and fear of judgment
- Support unavailable during crises (nights, weekends, emergencies)

**Psyflo democratizes mental wellness** by providing intelligent, personalized support anytime, anywhere.

## ✨ Key Features

- 📊 **AI-Powered Mood Tracking** - Log your mood (1-10 scale) with optional journal entries
- 🤖 **Personalized Recommendations** - Get AI-generated wellness suggestions based on your emotional state
- 💬 **Conversational AI Companion** - Real-time emotional support from Gemma-3-27b AI model
- 📈 **Mental Health Insights** - Track mood patterns and identify triggers over time
- 🔒 **Privacy-First Design** - Encrypted data, no external sharing, complete user control
- 🌙 **24/7 Accessibility** - Support available anytime, anywhere, judgment-free

## How to Use

### Step 1: Sign Up
1. Click **Sign Up**
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Create Account**

### Step 2: Log Your Mood
1. Go to **Dashboard**
2. Select your current mood (1-10 scale with emoji)
3. Optionally add notes about what you're feeling
4. Click **Save Mood**

### Step 3: Get AI Recommendations
The AI analyzes your mood and notes, then provides:
- Empathetic message understanding your feelings
- Personalized coping strategies
- Specific wellness suggestions based on your history

### Step 4: Track Your Progress
- View your **Mood History** to see patterns
- Check which activities help you most
- Identify mood triggers
- Celebrate improvements!

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js 15, React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend**: Firebase
- **AI**: Gemma-3-27b for emotion analysis and recommendations
- **Security**: Encrypted data, privacy-first architecture

## 🤖 How the AI Works

When you log your mood with notes:

1. **Your Input**
   - Mood level (1-10)
   - Optional notes: "feeling stressed about work"

2. **AI Analysis**
   - Detects emotions: stress, anxiety, pressure
   - Understands context: work-related challenge
   - Considers your history: what helped before

3. **Personalized Response**
   - Empathetic message: "I understand work stress..."
   - Specific suggestions: "Try a 5-minute walk"
   - Pattern insights: "You usually feel better after exercise"

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Is Psyflo free?**
A: Yes! Psyflo is completely free to use.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes. All data is encrypted and never shared. You control your privacy.

**Q: Can I use this instead of therapy?**
A: Psyflo complements therapy, not replaces it. Use it between sessions and for daily support.

**Q: How often should I log my mood?**
A: Daily is ideal, but any frequency helps. The more you log, the better AI understands you.

**Q: What if I'm not feeling the recommendations?**
A: Try different suggestions. The AI learns from your feedback. What works varies person to person.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Yes. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account. All your data will be removed.
